Chennai CCTV Locations Dataset

Promoting transparency, public safety, and civil liberties. 📞🌆🏞

Overview

This dataset archives the CCTV locations managed (which were managed) by the Greater Chennai City Police, initially published via the Android app com.hexcon.chennaicctvlocations (2019-2023). The data is being released into the public domain to promote transparency, enhance public safety, and ensure the accountability of surveillance systems. 📞🌆🏞

Dataset Contents

  • loc_id: Unique identifier for each CCTV location. 📍
  • loc_regid: Regional identifier associated with the location. 📍
  • loc_name: Name or description of the location. 🏠
  • loc_latitude: Latitude coordinate of the CCTV location. 🌐
  • loc_longitude: Longitude coordinate of the CCTV location. 🌍
  • loc_camera: Number of cameras at the location. 📷
  • loc_quality: Quality of the camera feed (e.g., HD, SD). 📹
  • loc_camstatus: Operational status of the camera (e.g., Active, Inactive). 🟢🔴
  • zone: Administrative or policing zone of the location. 🞮
  • district: District within Greater Chennai where the location resides. 📋
  • station: Police station responsible for the area. 🏢

The time of extraction of the dataset - April 2023, guessitmated updateness of data to be around 2021.

Purpose of Release

1. Enhancing Public Safety

By providing information on CCTV locations, the public can better understand the extent of surveillance coverage in their neighborhoods. This can help: 🔒🌍📹

  • Identify areas that need improved surveillance for crime prevention. 🕵️‍♂️
  • Optimize existing surveillance networks to support urban planning and security efforts. 🛠️

2. Improving Civil Liberties and Oversight

Publicly sharing this dataset ensures accountability in the use of CCTV cameras. Oversight mechanisms can be strengthened to: 🛡️🏛️🌇

  • Protect against misuse of surveillance systems. 🛑
  • Monitor the functionality and effectiveness of cameras. 🔍
  • Strike a balance between public safety and individual privacy. ⚖️

3. Encouraging Collaboration

Releasing this data empowers various stakeholders, including: 🔄🌎📚

  • Researchers to analyze trends in urban security. 📊
  • Civic organizations to advocate for responsible surveillance policies. 🤝
  • Technology developers to create tools that improve public awareness and safety. 💻

Terms of Use

Users are encouraged to utilize this dataset responsibly and respect the balance between public safety and privacy. ✨
